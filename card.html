Labrador Retriever

Back in the day Labrador Retrievers helped fisherman by pulling in boats.

  • Scientific Name: Canis Familiaris
  • Average Length: 22 to 24 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 10 to 12 years
  • Habitat: In a house on a pillow

Yellow, brown and black are the standard colors of labrador retrievers. They range between 55 and 10 pounds. They are one of America's most popular breeds.
